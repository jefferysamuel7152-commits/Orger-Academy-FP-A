# Orger Academy FP&A Model – Notes & Formulas

This file explains the key formulas and logic used in the FP&A model.

## 1. P&L Sheet

Revenue = Number of Customers × Price per Customer
Gross Profit = Revenue - Total Staff Cost
Operating Expenses = Rent (Annual) + Marketing
Operating Profit = Gross Profit - Operating Expenses
ROI = Operating Profit / Capital Investment
Payback Period = Investment/Operating Profit

## 2. Revenue Sheet

- Revenue is calculated monthly and summed for Year 1.
- Forecast assumptions are based on average student strength and pricing per location.

## 3. Customer Acquisition

- Admissions increase modeled monthly.
- Target Market = Capturable Market × Conversion Rate.

## 4. Expense Sheets

- Labor, marketing, and operational costs are broken down per month.
- Costs are annualized in the P&L sheet for clarity.

## 5. CapEx & Capital

- Furniture, decor, and initial investments are listed in the Capital sheet.
- CapEx totals feed into ROI and Payback calculations.
